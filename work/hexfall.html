HEXFALL

Tactical roguelike on a hex grid. Designed turn economy, three enemy archetypes, and the loot-curve simulator.

PROJECT OVERVIEW

HEXFALL is a tactical roguelike played on a hexagonal grid. The project involved designing a complete turn economy, creating three distinct enemy archetypes with unique behavior trees, and building a loot-curve simulator to tune reward pacing across 50+ procedural encounters.

The vertical slice demonstrates the full gameplay loop: explore, encounter, loot, upgrade. Each hex tile carries terrain modifiers that interact with the combat system, creating emergent tactical depth.
