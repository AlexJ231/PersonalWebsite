THE LOOP_ECONOMY

A four-year R&D thread on player retention curves, dopamine pacing and reward timing.

PROJECT OVERVIEW

THE LOOP_ECONOMY is a four-year research thread investigating the mechanics behind player retention. The project models dopamine pacing, reward timing, and engagement loops through simulation and real-world playtest data.

The open-source design document and accompanying web-based simulator allow other designers to model their own reward curves and predict player drop-off points before committing to production.
