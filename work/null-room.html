PROTOTYPE · 2024

NULL//ROOM

Spatial puzzle game built around acoustic propagation. The player solves rooms by listening, not looking.

PROJECT OVERVIEW

NULL//ROOM strips away visual cues and replaces them with spatial audio. Players navigate dark rooms using only sound — echo, reverb, and frequency shifts reveal the geometry of each puzzle space.

The prototype explores how acoustic propagation can replace traditional level design signposting. Every surface has a material that affects sound reflection, creating a playable soundscape.
