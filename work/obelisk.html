First-person climbing simulator with procedural geology. Released on Steam, 92% positive over 4k reviews.

PROJECT OVERVIEW

OBELISK is a first-person climbing simulator where the terrain is generated through procedural geology. Rock faces, crevices, and ledges emerge from layered noise functions that simulate real geological strata.

The game shipped on Steam to 92% positive reviews over 4,000 ratings. Players praised the physicality of the climbing mechanics and the meditative quality of the procedural landscapes.
